Product Bar Chart(Retail Sale)

Number of Customer In Area, Bubble chart

With this Bubble chart I want to show the locations of customers in Ahmedabad City
With this graph we can see that at what area of City there are more number of customers and what area there are less. based on this we can make certain decision like in what area shop has to put more commercial or target customer. as well it helps in making decision if shop person want to open another branch for the shop.
Prof.Koop suggested me to set the opacity less for the bigger bubble that indicates that at particular area there are high volume of customers but it should not hide the other bubble under it, that's how we can get better information with making other bubble visible as well. two rectangle show the location for shop.







Sale Line Chart

With this line chart I try to show how the sale has been trough out the year.
this graph also support reality as in every year during the summer time in april, may, june the sale decrease because the cloths for summer are not much expensive, as well summer cloths are not shop's popular product. during the july, aug sale increase at this time all shops give more discount and many scheme with the sale. During the september sale decrease because already shop's raguler customers has visited the shop recently in discount and scheme time. through out october to january sale increase just in between november sale decrease because the shop remain close for 7-8 days for DIWALI festival
